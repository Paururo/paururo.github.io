Pathotypr preprint is out on bioRxiv!

Our preprint “Pathotypr: harmonised MTBC lineage assignment and resistance-associated variant detection for genomic surveillance” is now available on bioRxiv. Pathotypr is an alignment-free tool that supports all 14 currently recognised MTBC lineages and WHO catalogue-based resistance calling, processing ~1 sample/second. Validated on 88,071 samples with 100% lineage concordance and high resistance prediction performance.
